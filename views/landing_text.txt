<p>
Hydrologic Micro Services, HMS, is a watershed modeling system comprised of hydrology, erosion, and contaminant fate
and transport components. Data preparation is included with automated data downloads from national and local data sources and pre-processing data for consumption by models.
By tapping into the strengths of the different approaches taken by different models, HMS can link air and water models together to address more complex scenarios.

Hydrologic Micro Services, HMS, is a watershed modeling system comprised of data provisioning and modeling components and workflows to facilitate environmental modeling.
The components are available as <a href="https://qedinternal.epa.gov/hms/api_doc/">web services</a>, <a href="https://github.com/quanted/hms">desktop libraries</a>, and through a <a href="https://qedinternal.epa.gov/hms/api_doc/">web-based graphical user interface</a>.
The target users for HMS are hydrological and water quality modelers and analysts.
HMS users can use the system components and workflows directly or build their own workflows by linking the components.

</p>
